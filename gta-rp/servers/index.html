Methodology

How each rating is built.

Three axes: PLA compliance, marketing-vs-reality, transition readiness. Each axis scored independently with evidence. Full rubric, weighting, and evidence handling published in the methodology spec. Ratings update quarterly or when a documented shift justifies it.

Right of reply

Servers contacted before publication.

Every server rated in The Scoreboard is contacted before publication. Responses published alongside ratings in full. A partial rating with a credible, documented response gets re-reviewed publicly. The Scoreboard is editorial; editorial means being willing to listen.

A note on inclusion

The directory is not comprehensive by design. It covers UK GTA RP servers with the scale, track record, or editorial relevance to matter. Popularity is indicative, not a quality signal — The Map and The Scoreboard separate popularity from quality explicitly. Servers that shut down, rebrand, or fall out of relevance move to The Ledger. Servers can write in to be considered for inclusion, but the editorial bar is set independently of volume or lobbying.
