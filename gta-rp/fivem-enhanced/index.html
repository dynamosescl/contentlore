Technical Overview

What Is FiveM Enhanced?

FiveM Enhanced is a platform upgrade bringing improved performance, better anti-cheat, enhanced scripting APIs, and native voice chat improvements. It requires server-side migration and may break existing scripts that rely on deprecated APIs.

Impact on UK Servers

UK RP servers face varying migration complexity depending on their custom script ecosystem. Larger servers with extensive custom frameworks (JESTRP, ONERP) require more testing time. Smaller servers may migrate faster but face resource constraints.

Creator Experience

For streamers, the transition is largely transparent — improved performance and stability. However, during migration windows, some servers may experience downtime or reduced features, potentially affecting stream quality and viewer experience.

Timeline Outlook

The transition is ongoing with no hard deadline. Servers are migrating at their own pace. ContentLore tracks each server's stated plans and observed progress to give the community clear visibility on where things stand.
