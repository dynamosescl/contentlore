GTA 6

Strategic briefing on GTA 6's impact on the UK GTA RP ecosystem. Scenarios, community preparedness, and transition planning.
Key Questions
Will FiveM Work with GTA 6?
Rockstar's acquisition of Cfx.re (FiveM's parent company) in 2023 signals official RP support may come natively. However, no formal announcement has been made regarding GTA 6 mod support. The community expects a wait of 12-24 months post-launch before any RP framework emerges.
What Happens to GTA 5 RP?
GTA 5 RP will likely continue for 1-3 years after GTA 6 launches. The FiveM ecosystem is mature with thousands of servers and scripts. A gradual migration rather than a sudden switch is the most probable outcome.
How Will Viewership Shift?
Initial GTA 6 launch will pull significant viewership to vanilla content. RP viewership may dip 30-50% temporarily. UK creators with diversified content strategies are better positioned to navigate the transition.
Should UK Servers Prepare Now?
Yes. Server owners should focus on community retention, diversified revenue streams, and maintaining creator relationships. Technical preparation can wait until a modding framework is confirmed.

Transition Scenarios
Best Case

Smooth Coexistence

GTA 6 mod tools arrive within 12 months. UK servers migrate communities intact. FiveM Enhanced bridges the gap. Viewership dips briefly then recovers as GTA 6 RP matures. UK scene emerges stronger with better technology.

Most Likely

Extended Parallel

GTA 6 modding takes 18-24 months. GTA 5 RP continues with reduced but stable viewership. Some creators split time between both. UK servers maintain operations but reduce development investment. Gradual transition over 2+ years.

Worst Case

Fragmentation

No GTA 6 modding support announced. GTA 5 RP viewership drops sharply. UK servers struggle with reduced player counts. Community fragments across different games. Recovery depends on GTA 6 eventually getting mod support.
