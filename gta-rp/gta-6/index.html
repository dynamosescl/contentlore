GTA 6

How the next generation of Grand Theft Auto will reshape the UK RP ecosystem. What we know, what it means, and how servers and creators are preparing.

GTA 6 is confirmed and will fundamentally change the RP landscape. The core question for the UK community isn't whether GTA 5 RP will survive — it's how the transition plays out. This page tracks developments and analyses impact through a UK RP lens.

01

Will FiveM Work with GTA 6?

No official confirmation. Cfx.re (FiveM's parent) has not announced GTA 6 mod framework support. The community expects a significant gap between GTA 6 launch and any RP mod framework. Historical precedent: FiveM took years to mature after GTA 5.

02

What Happens to GTA 5 RP?

GTA 5 RP will likely continue for 12–24+ months after GTA 6 launches. Server investments, established communities, and the absence of a GTA 6 mod framework mean the transition will be gradual, not overnight.

03

How Will Viewership Shift?

Expect a major viewership spike for GTA 6 content at launch, pulling viewers from GTA 5 RP. UK RP viewership may dip 30–50% initially before stabilising. Creators who diversify early will be best positioned.

04

Should UK Servers Prepare Now?

Yes — but for continuity, not migration. The smart play is reinforcing community bonds, diversifying content, and ensuring the server experience is strong enough to retain players through the transition period.

Transition Scenarios

Best Case

Smooth Coexistence

GTA 6 mod tools arrive within 12 months. UK servers migrate communities intact. FiveM Enhanced bridges the gap. Viewership dips briefly then recovers as GTA 6 RP matures. UK scene emerges stronger with better technology.

Most Likely

Extended Parallel

GTA 6 modding takes 18–24 months. GTA 5 RP continues with reduced but stable viewership. Some creators split time between both. UK servers maintain operations but reduce development investment. Gradual transition over 2+ years.

Worst Case

Fragmentation

No GTA 6 modding support announced. GTA 5 RP viewership drops sharply. UK servers struggle with reduced player counts. Community fragments across different games. Recovery depends on GTA 6 eventually getting mod support.
