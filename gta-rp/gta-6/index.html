Tracked servers stay on FiveM through launch

None of the 13 RP servers we track have published a GTA 6 transition plan. Default posture is "carry on with FiveM" — sensible given GTA 6 has no PC date and Project ROME is unconfirmed. Real movement is unlikely before late 2027 at earliest.

Transition Scenarios 12–24 month outlook
Best Case

Smooth Coexistence

Project ROME ships within 6–12 months of GTA 6 launch with proper server tooling. Tracked servers migrate communities intact while keeping FiveM as a fallback. Viewership dips briefly then compounds — UK scene emerges with two storefronts instead of one.

Most Likely

Extended Parallel

GTA 6 PC arrives 12–18 months post-console (late 2027 / early 2028). Modding tools follow another 12+ months. UK GTA 5 RP runs alongside vanilla GTA 6 viewership for 2+ years. Creators split time. Viewership floor is lower, but the scene survives.

Worst Case

Locked-Down Platform

Rockstar enforces tighter ToS via the Cfx.re Platform Licence. Project ROME is creator-monetisation-first, not RP-first. Tracked servers face higher compliance burden, smaller creators get squeezed, and a chunk of the scene migrates to alternate game RP (ArmA, Cyberpunk RP, etc.).
