The Desk · Long essay

19 November 2026. GTA 6 has a date. The UK scene has seven months — not eighteen.

Rockstar confirmed the launch window in November 2025 and reconfirmed in February 2026. Take-Two has already shut down alt:V. NoPixel V will launch on the Rockstar Games Launcher. Datamined evidence points to ROME — the Rockstar Online Modding Engine — eventually replacing FiveM. Anyone covering UK streaming has until this November to build authority that the next scene cannot replicate. The runway is shorter than anyone's acting like.
