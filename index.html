New to UK GTA RP?

UK GTA RP is improvised drama — British creators play characters living, working and getting into trouble inside modded GTA V servers. Story arcs run for weeks. Plotlines cross creators. Servers behave like neighbourhoods.

01 What it is

"FiveM" is a mod for Grand Theft Auto V that lets people host private servers with custom rules. Roleplay servers run scripts that simulate jobs, police, courts, gangs and economies. Streamers stay in-character (IC) for hours at a time — they're not playing themselves, they're playing someone living in Los Santos.

Stream titles like "Day 47 — court hearing for the Anchor Bay heist" aren't a joke. That's a continuing storyline.

02 Where it happens

UK creators play across thirteen tracked servers. Some are British-founded, some are American servers with strong UK presence:

  • UK-builtUnique, TNG, Orbit, Drill UK, British Life, The Ends
  • US w/ UK sceneNew Era, Prodigy, D10, Chase, Unmatched
  • MixedVeraRP, Let's RP

Quick glossary
IC / OOC"In character" vs. "out of character". Streamers stay IC unless they're answering chat directly.
CharacterThe persona a streamer plays. Dynamoses on Kick plays multiple characters across servers; their character names rarely match their handle.
ServerA single FiveM world with its own rules, jobs, factions and economy. Characters can't cross servers.
WhitelistMost quality RP servers require an application. That's why you can't just join the server you're watching.
Raid / HostOne streamer redirecting their viewers to another. Often deliberate — friends pointing at friends.
Story arcA multi-stream plot, sometimes spanning weeks. Heists, court cases, gang wars, romance lines.
