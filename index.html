What Is ContentLore

ContentLore is an independent streaming intelligence platform focused on the UK GTA RP ecosystem. We track live streams, detect scenes, curate editorial content, and provide the community with tools and context no one else offers.

How It Works

We poll Twitch and Kick every 15 minutes, capturing live status, viewers, and stream metadata for thousands of creators. Scene detection groups concurrent streams by server. Editorial layers add context through beef tracking and lore curation.

Open & Independent

ContentLore operates independently with full editorial transparency. Read our code of ethics, check the ledger, or get in touch. Built on Cloudflare's edge network for speed everywhere.
